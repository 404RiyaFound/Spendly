Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we also store the expense records you add — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and maintaining your account.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical precautions — including encrypted storage and access controls — to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) to operate the app; these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and may not use your data for their own purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, please reach out using the contact details below.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us via the details provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 5 business days.
